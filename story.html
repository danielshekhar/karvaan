My Story

Learn about how Karvaan came to be and the passion behind it.

Hi, I am Kanishk, the founder of Karvaan, an edtech start-up that allows everyone to pursue their dreams at no cost, and this is my story.

From childhood, I was interested in different fields. When I used to watch a show named "Adalat," it sparked my interest in law, and I wanted to become a lawyer. However, one day I watched a movie called "Genius" that caught my attention and led me to the world of Cybersecurity. I began to dream of becoming a hacker. Eventually, my interest shifted to Computer Science, and after completing my 10th grade, I decided to drop out and pursue self-paced learning. I knew about resources like MIT OpenCourseWare and NIOS that allowed me to continue my education professionally.

While on this journey, I realized that many people had dreams, but the education available to them was either very poor or prohibitively expensive. This inspired me to solve this problem and create a platform where people could gain the skills they loved, completely for free. This led to the creation of Karvaan, a platform where anyone can learn anything, at no cost.

Throughout this journey, there was one person who stood by me from the beginning to the end—my father, Mr. Mahendra Singh Shekhawat. He supported every decision I made, and this success and Karvaan are dedicated to him.

- Kanishk Mahendra
